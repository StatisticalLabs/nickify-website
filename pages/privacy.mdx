# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we at Statistical Labs collect, use, store, and share your information. Use of Nickify and it's services is considered an agreement to the terms in this Policy.

## What data does Nickify store?

- Guild IDs
- User IDs
- YouTube Channel IDs

## Why does Nickify need and use this data?

1. Guild IDs are stored to know which guild a user's nickname should be updated on.

2. User IDs are stored to know what user's nickname should be updated.

3. YouTube Channel IDs are stored to keep track of it's subscriber count and update the user's nickname when needed.

## Does Nickify share our data with any 3rd parties?

No, we don't share data with any 3rd parties. So you'll be sure your data won't appear on the dark web!

## How can users contact the bot owner?

Users can join our [Discord server](/discord) and contact us through there.
